1️⃣ Why is load shedding happening?

→ Eskom cannot generate enough electricity.

2️⃣ Why can’t Eskom generate enough electricity?

→ Aging infrastructure, corruption, and breakdowns have reduced capacity.

3️⃣ Why hasn’t the issue been fixed?

→ Poor government planning and lack of investment in alternative energy sources.

4️⃣ Why aren’t more people switching to alternative power?

→ Solar and backup systems are too expensive for most households and businesses.

5️⃣ Why are costs so high?

→ High import tariffs, lack of subsidies, and monopolization of energy solutions.
